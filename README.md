# Combo Tracker

Combo Tracker is a straightforward Android application designed for Trading Card Game (TCG) players to digitally document, track, and practice their deck combos. Users can create step-by-step lists for their complex plays, attach reference images of specific cards to individual steps, and check off their progress in real-time. It serves as a customizable, offline digital notebook to keep card interactions organized and easily accessible during practice sessions or deck building.
